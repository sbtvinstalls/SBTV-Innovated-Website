Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an installation take?

A standard installation on drywall typically takes between 60 to 90 minutes. Mounting on more complex surfaces like brick or above a fireplace may take longer.

Do I need to provide my own mount?

You can provide your own mount, or we can supply a high-quality, professional-grade mount for you. We carry a variety of fixed, tilting, and full-motion mounts to suit your needs.

Will you hide the cables?

Yes, in-wall wire concealment is one of our most popular add-on services. We can route all your cables through the wall for a clean, professional look with no visible wires.
